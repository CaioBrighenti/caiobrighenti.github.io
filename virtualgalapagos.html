Stereo Opus

November 2016 - Present

Stereo Opus is a small virtual reality studio I co-founded along with a colleague at Colgate University. Our official website can be found here. Our mission is to produce high quality virtual reality content for high end hardware. My goal is to leverage the potential of story telling in virtual reality by building detailed experiences that are designed from the ground up for the platform. We are currently developing one major project, Decide. Decide puts the player in the shoes of a man down on his luck, forced to deal with his inner demons. As the experience, or dream as we call it, progresses, the player is faced with a series difficult decisions, ultimately affecting the conclusion of the story.
